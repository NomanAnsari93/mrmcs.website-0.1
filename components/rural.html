MRMCS Rural Economy & Agricultural Development

Our vision is to run Rural Economy & Agricultural Development Centre (READC) in all remote villages and in taluka headquarters of Marathwada Region,in 1st phase we planned to run in Pathri Sector, Parbhani District Maharashtra State India. READ Centre will support small farmers ( To Grow More Food Campaign) by providing latest technology, Agricultural equipment and tractors for cultivation and harvesting on hire, hourly bases, on 50% discount, Warehouses, cold storages, sterilizing units, Mini vegetable markets, Food processing units, Dairy farms and milk collection centers, Goat & Sheep farms, Fish ponds, Aqua culture, Siri culture and Horticulture, Sea food stalls and MRMCS Canteens in all selected villages of Pathri Sector, and we run free Residential Schools, Dispensaries, Hospitals, Yoga and Meditation Centers, Old Age Homes, Sports, Games and Libraries Under READ Centre in all villages and taluka headquarters of Pathri Sector in 1st phase. And we run Agri stores, Auto Engineering Work Shops, Auto Garages, Auto Spare parts stores ( for all vehicles), water service stations, Drinking mineral water plants, Call Taxi services, Small financial & Micro Finance Services in all blocks of Pathri Sector. And we proposed and planned to take 25 Acers of Agriculture land for lease jointly with small farmers in each block to cultivate and harvest through out Marathwada Region, Maharashtra State, India by 23 - 28 .
